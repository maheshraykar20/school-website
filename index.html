Shri Mayureshwar English Medium School, Pohegaon

Premier institution dedicated to providing quality education since its establishment in the academic year 2009-10. Founded by our esteemed chairman, Mr. Vilasrao Paraji Salunke, and Mrs. Vaishali Vilas Salunke, our school began its journey with a vision to nurture young minds in a conducive learning environment. Under the able administration of Principal Mrs. Kavita Ravindra Vise, has grown into a thriving educational institution committed to academic excellence, holistic development, and a deep-rooted sense of values. From humble beginnings with just 23 students, we have expanded our reach and impact, enriching the lives of countless students over the years. Our dedication to providing a safe, natural, and culturally rich environment sets us apart, ensuring that every child receives a well-rounded education that prepares them for the challenges of tomorrow. At Shri. Mayureshwar English Medium we celebrate diversity, foster creativity, and instill a lifelong love for learning. Join us as we continue to inspire and empower the leaders of tomorrow.

Phonics

Phonics involves matching the sounds of spoken English with individual letters or groups of letters. Teaching children to blend the sounds of letters together helps them decode unfamiliar or unknown words by sounding them out.



Sanskrit
